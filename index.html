⚡ Tech Insight

The Hidden Cost of Lazy Loading: While lazy loading improves initial page load times, overusing it can actually hurt user experience and SEO. Search engines may not fully crawl lazily-loaded content if JavaScript execution fails, and users scrolling quickly might trigger cascade loads that feel janky.

Best practice: Load above-the-fold critical content eagerly, lazy-load below the fold in batches with a slight delay (100-200ms) after scroll stops to create smoother perceived performance.
